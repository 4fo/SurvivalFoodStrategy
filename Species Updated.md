### **High Altitude Strategy**

**High-Altitude Western Ghats (Kodaikanal/Palani Hills, \~2000m+)**

Cool climate (15-25°C days, cool nights, mist, occasional frost, acidic soils). Focus on temperate hill crops \+ adapted subtropicals.

**1\. Fuel / Calorie-Rich Staples**

* Primary: **Potatoes** (major local crop).  
* Additions: Sweet Potatoes, local Dioscorea yams (protected spots), carrots, beetroot.

**2\. Protein / Soil Enrichers**

* Primary: French beans, broad/faba beans.  
* Additions: Rice Bean (hardy up to 2000m), cowpea, chickpeas in cooler seasons. Limited Lablab in milder microclimates.

**3\. Bulk Food / Long Storage**

* Primary: **Chow-Chow (Chayote)** – thrives locally.  
* Additions: Pumpkins (protected), root crops (carrot, beetroot, garlic).

**4\. Hardy Greens / Vitamins**

* Kale, Swiss chard, spinach, cabbage, amaranth, wild ferns, local mustards/chenopodiums. Limited Malabar spinach.

**5\. Backup / Survival**

* Jerusalem Artichoke (trial – suits cooler temps), local Dioscorea yams, chow-chow vines, perennial beans.

**Summary (High Alt Only):**

* Calories: Potatoes, Sweet Potato, local yams.  
* Protein: French beans, Rice Bean, Broad Beans.  
* Storage: Chow-Chow, root crops, pumpkins.  
* Vitamins: Kale, chard, spinach, cabbage, amaranth, wild greens.  
* Backup: Jerusalem Artichoke, Dioscorea yams, chow-chow.

### **Low Altitude Strategy**

**Lowland / Plains South Indian**

Hot, humid tropical (28-38°C, monsoon patterns).

**1\. Fuel / Calorie-Rich Staples**

* Primary: **Tapioca / Cassava**.  
* Additions: Sweet Potato, Elephant Foot Yam, Greater/Lesser Yams, Plantain/Banana.

**2\. Protein / Soil Enrichers**

* Primary: **Pigeon Pea**.  
* Additions: Lablab (Avarai), Cluster Beans, Cowpea, Rice Bean.

**3\. Bulk Food / Long Storage**

* Primary: Ash Gourd, Bottle Gourd, Pumpkin, Snake/Ridge Gourd.  
* Additions: Elephant Foot Yam, Cassava tubers.

**4\. Hardy Greens / Vitamins**

* Primary: **Moringa**.  
* Additions: Malabar Spinach, Amaranth, Chekurmanis, Curry leaves, holy basil, wild greens.

**5\. Backup / Survival**

* Elephant Foot Yam, Cassava, Pigeon Pea, Moringa, Lablab.

**Summary (Lowland Only):**

* Calories: Cassava, Sweet Potato, Yams, Plantain.  
* Protein: Pigeon Pea, Lablab, Cluster Bean, Cowpea.  
* Storage: Ash Gourd, Pumpkins, Gourds, Yams.  
* Vitamins: Moringa, Malabar Spinach, Amaranth.  
* Backup: Elephant Foot Yam, Cassava, Moringa.

### **High Altitude Strategy (Kodaikanal / Palani Hills \~2000m+)**

Cool, misty, occasional frost, acidic soils. Expanded with proven hill crops.

**1\. Fuel / Calorie-Rich Staples**

* Primary: **Potatoes** (including local varieties).  
* Strong additions: Sweet Potatoes (adapted varieties), Carrots, Beetroot, Garlic (GI-tagged Kodaikanal hill garlic), Hill Banana (Virupakshi), Turnips, Radishes.

**2\. Protein / Soil Enrichers**

* Primary: French Beans, Broad/Faba Beans.  
* Additions: Rice Bean, Cowpea (cooler seasons), Chickpeas, Peas, Runner Beans/Scarlet Runner (perennial potential).

**3\. Bulk Food / Long Storage**

* Primary: **Chow-Chow (Chayote)** — highly prolific locally.  
* Additions: Pumpkins (protected microclimates), Garlic (excellent storage), Carrots/Beetroot, Cabbage (stores well), Avocado (as calorie-dense tree crop).

**4\. Hardy Greens / Vitamins (Cut-and-Come-Again)**

* Kale, Swiss Chard, Spinach, Cabbage, Amaranth (local varieties), Mustard Greens, Local Chenopodiums/Wild Mustards, Fiddlehead Ferns (foraged/wild), Sorrel.  
* Herbs: Coriander, Mint, Holy Basil (protected), Thyme/Oregano (perennial).

**5\. Backup / Survival (Set-and-Forget)**

* Jerusalem Artichoke (suits cool climate), Local Dioscorea Yams (protected/wild relatives), Chow-Chow vines (perennial), Perennial Beans (e.g., Runner), Avocado and Hill Banana (long-term trees).

**Summary (High Altitude Only)**

* **Calories**: Potatoes, Carrots, Beetroot, Sweet Potato, Hill Banana, Garlic.  
* **Protein**: French Beans, Broad Beans, Rice Bean, Peas, Cowpea.  
* **Storage/Bulk**: Chow-Chow, Root crops (carrot, beet, garlic), Cabbage, Pumpkins.  
* **Vitamins**: Kale, Chard, Spinach, Cabbage, Amaranth, Mustard greens, Wild ferns.  
* **Backup**: Jerusalem Artichoke, Dioscorea yams, Chow-Chow, Perennial legumes, Avocado.

### **Lowland / Plains Strategy (Tamil Nadu / Kerala lowlands)**

Hot, humid tropical, monsoon-driven. Expanded with traditional resilient perennials.

**1\. Fuel / Calorie-Rich Staples**

* Primary: **Tapioca / Cassava**.  
* Strong additions: Sweet Potato (Sakkaravalli), Elephant Foot Yam (Senai/Suran), Greater/Lesser Yams (Dioscorea), Plantain/Banana (multiple varieties), Minor Millets (Ragi, Varagu, etc. for diversity).

**2\. Protein / Soil Enrichers**

* Primary: **Pigeon Pea (Toor Dal)** — perennial shrub.  
* Additions: Lablab (Avarai), Cluster Beans (Kothavarai), Cowpea (Karamani), Rice Bean, Sword Bean, Winged Bean, Green Gram/Black Gram (quick cycles).

**3\. Bulk Food / Long Storage**

* Primary: **Ash Gourd**, Bottle Gourd, Pumpkin.  
* Additions: Snake Gourd, Ridge Gourd, Elephant Foot Yam, Cassava tubers, Jackfruit (tree-level bulk).

**4\. Hardy Greens / Vitamins**

* Primary: **Moringa (Murungai)**.  
* Additions: Malabar Spinach (Basella), Amaranth (multiple local types — Thandu Keerai etc.), Chekurmanis (Sauropus androgynus), Curry Leaves, Holy Basil, Ceylon Spinach, Katuk, Brazilian Spinach (perennial), various wild/foraged greens.

**5\. Backup / Survival**

* Elephant Foot Yam, Cassava, Pigeon Pea (perennial), Moringa (drought-tolerant tree), Lablab (persistent climber), Jackfruit (ultra-reliable tree).

**Summary (Lowland Only)**

* **Calories**: Cassava, Sweet Potato, Yams, Plantain/Banana, Minor Millets.  
* **Protein**: Pigeon Pea, Lablab, Cluster Bean, Cowpea, Sword Bean.  
* **Storage/Bulk**: Ash Gourd family, Pumpkins/Gourds, Yams, Jackfruit.  
* **Vitamins**: Moringa, Malabar Spinach, Amaranth, Chekurmanis, Curry Leaves.  
* **Backup**: Elephant Foot Yam, Cassava, Moringa, Pigeon Pea, Jackfruit.
